01 Pillar One

Systems — The Right Tools, All Connected

A construction business running on disconnected software is constantly leaking time, money, and leads. The Systems pillar is about getting your tech stack working as one unified platform — with GoHighLevel as the hub and everything else feeding into it.

Featured Tool Inside This Pillar

Build Bridge™
Construction Platform Integrations

Build Bridge is our purpose-built app on the GHL Marketplace that connects the construction software your business is already running on — and introduces the tools that fill your gaps. Whatever you're using, we connect it to your Synergy CRM so everything talks to each other and nothing falls through the cracks.

When a customer configures a building in Idearoom, it becomes a lead in GHL automatically. When a SmartBuild quote is sent, it moves your pipeline. When a deal closes, Monday.com — which we set up for you — creates the project. Your software stops being islands and starts being a system.

02 Pillar Two

Processes — A Sales System Your Team Can Follow

Great systems mean nothing if your team doesn't have a repeatable process for turning leads into customers. The Processes pillar gives your salespeople a proven playbook — built on the ALS Framework™ — so every customer interaction moves forward with confidence.

The Sales Foundation

ALS Framework™

Ask. Listen. Solve. Three words that form the most effective construction sales process we've ever built. It's not about pressure or pitching — it's about genuinely understanding what the customer needs and presenting the right solution every time.

A
Ask
Ask the right questions to uncover what the customer actually wants — not just what they say they want. Great discovery is the foundation of every closed deal.
L
Listen
Most salespeople are too busy thinking about what to say next. The ALS Framework trains your team to truly hear the customer — their concerns, priorities, and buying signals.
S
Solve
Present your solution in terms of their specific situation. When you've asked and listened first, solving feels natural — and closing feels easy.

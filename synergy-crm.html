The Problem

Generic CRMs Weren't Built for the Way Builders Sell

If you've tried HubSpot, Salesforce, or a spreadsheet — you know what's missing.

Leads slip through the cracks.

You get a Facebook message Saturday morning. By Monday, they've bought from the competitor down the road.

Follow-up is all manual.

You know follow-up closes deals. You also know there aren't enough hours in the day to do it by hand.

Your tools don't talk to each other.

SmartBuild, QuickBooks, Monday.com, your phone, your email — each one holds a piece of the puzzle.

You're the bottleneck.

Every quote, every follow-up call, every review request goes through you. The business stops when you stop.

The Solution

A CRM That Works the Way You Build

Synergy CRM is the centerpiece of the Streamlined Construction System™ — one platform that captures leads, runs your follow-up, manages your pipeline, and connects the tools you already use. Built on the ALS Framework™ (Ask. Listen. Solve.), it's not just software — it's a sales system.

What's Inside

Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

Seven features that turn your sales process from reactive to repeatable.

Built for Builders

Every field, pipeline, and workflow is designed for the way shed, post frame, and barndominium businesses actually sell. No forcing construction into software made for SaaS companies.

Lead Capture + ALS Follow-Up

Leads from your website, Facebook, Marketplace, and walk-ins all land in one place — and trigger follow-up sequences built on the ALS Framework™ (Ask. Listen. Solve.) so every prospect feels heard, not hounded.

Pipeline Management

Track quotes, deposits, builds, and deliveries in visual pipelines. See exactly where every opportunity stands — and what needs to happen next to move it forward.

SMS, Email & Voicemail Drops — in One Place

Send a text, schedule an email, and drop a ringless voicemail from the same contact record. Two-way conversations stay organized, searchable, and assigned to the right salesperson.

Review Generation

Automated review requests trigger after delivery — so the customers who love your work are the ones filling up Google and Facebook. Your reputation grows while you're building the next barn.

Integrations That Matter

Connect the tools you already use: SmartBuild for quoting, QuickBooks for accounting, Monday.com for project management. Synergy CRM becomes the hub — not another silo.

Done-For-You Setup

You don't have time to configure a CRM. We build your pipelines, workflows, and automations for you — based on 18 years of construction sales experience. You show up to a system that's already running.

Current Integrations

The Tools We Connect — Starting Here, Growing Fast

These are the platforms we've built deep, purpose-built connections for. Don't see yours? Book a call — we're expanding the library and want to know what you're running.

SB
SmartBuild
Configure, Price & Quote

SmartBuild is where your building configurations and quotes live. This integration pulls that data directly into GHL — so every quote, every customer, and every building spec is visible in your CRM pipeline without anyone having to copy and paste.

  • New quotes auto-create or update contacts in GHL
  • Building specs synced to the contact record
  • Quote status changes trigger GHL pipeline moves
  • Quote values populate deal values in CRM
  • Automated follow-up sequences triggered from quote activity
IR
Idearoom
3D Building Configurator

When a customer designs their building in Idearoom, that's a high-intent lead. This integration captures those configurator sessions and brings them into GHL as hot leads — complete with their design specs attached — so your team can follow up fast.

  • Configurator sessions captured as GHL leads instantly
  • Building design data attached to contact record
  • High-intent trigger fires follow-up automation
  • Design link shared with sales rep automatically
  • Customer design history visible in contact timeline
QB
QuickBooks
Accounting & Invoicing Coming Soon

Stop toggling between your CRM and accounting software. When a deal closes in GHL, QuickBooks gets updated. When an invoice is paid, your CRM knows about it. Your sales and finance teams finally have the same picture.

  • Won deals in GHL create customers in QuickBooks
  • Invoice creation triggered from GHL pipeline stages
  • Payment received status synced back to CRM
  • Revenue reporting connected to GHL contacts
  • Customer billing history visible in GHL
Mo
Monday.com
Project Management — We Introduce This Coming Soon

Most builders don't have a project management system in place when they come to us — and that's completely normal. As part of the Streamlined Construction System, we recommend and configure Monday.com for your production team. Once it's set up, Build Bridge™ connects it directly to GHL so every closed deal automatically creates a new project board — and your production team always knows exactly what's coming.

  • We set up and configure Monday.com for your team
  • Closed deals in GHL trigger new project boards automatically
  • Customer and building data pre-populated on every board
  • Project milestone updates sync back to GHL contact timeline
  • Sales and production finally working from the same information
